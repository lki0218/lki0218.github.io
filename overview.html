Overview

This is a Player vs. Player (PvP) game with two players, one controlling the zombie and the other controlling the human. To control the zombie, use the keys a,w,s,d. The keys up, down, right, and left are used to control the human's movement. The goal of this game is to chase each other in the provided space; when the characters touch, the points increase until they are separated. The game will end when the score reaches 150.
